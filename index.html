ABOUT US

Renaissance School of Science and Technology is founded on the belief that every child deserves an education as a vehicle for their self-enrichment. When the Renaissance School of Science and Technology opened its gates in the beginning it catered to the children of its faculty, employees, and those referred by kind-hearted benefactors in the town of Morong, province of Rizal Strongly, The School grew as it accepted more students from the neighboring towns of Cardona, Binangonan, Baras, Teresa, and Pililla, all in Rizal, among others.
For years, the School struggled against the raising of operating the school. However, the founder/chairwoman of RSST kept the school afloat with her own personal resources.
As the school complied with the continuous improvements and updated policies of the Department of Education. RSST managed to complete its curricula k to 12 offerings that now include:

Pre-School
Basic Education
Junior High School
Senior High School
College Courses
3-Year Diploma Courses
TESDA Courses

As the school continued to upgrade its physical facilities, it also evolved in providing its students state of the art gadgets required to meet the academic standards, especially during the time of the pandemic that required an online method of learning.
Our student population continues to grow to offer the availability of scholarships, grants, and government subsidies such as Tulong Dunong and the practical offer in the TESDA courses such as Housekeeping, Barista, Events Management, and Cookery among others. Thousands of out of school youth, housewives, retirees, and single moms and dads found employment as a result of getting their training from said TESDA courses.
Today, RSST is happy to announce that it has maintained its version of helping the marginalized sector of our society to find gainful employment or set up their SMEs Small enterprises and turn become archive contributors to the community development
